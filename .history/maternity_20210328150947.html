Oheze Naka: Health

Oheze naka community lack basic health facilities, the only available health center in the community is the maternity built by the Indigenes in 1972.At the opening,it has staff strength of three midwives, two auxiliary nurse, and two labourers but for the past eighteen years now only one nurse operates the maternity which is now of a chemist than maternity. The nurse only sell drugs to the expectant mothers at exuberant price because the government provide none.Our people now patronize traditional nurse at moderate prices.The center need urgent revival to the days of old.
