Oheze Naka: Economy

Occupation

Farming is the main occupation. Rearing of domestic animals is also practiced by the adults. Farm Produce include Yam, Plantain, Bananas, Palm Produce, Pineapples, Cassava, Fruits, Vegetables etc. Shifting cultivation is the main strategy for soil management.
